Ski Touring

Ski touring, a popular winter activity, combines the thrill of downhill skiing with the adventure of back country exploration. Unlike traditional downhill skiing, ski touring involves ascending slopes using specialized skis with climbing skins attached to the base, allowing skiers to traverse uphill terrain efficiently. The ascent is often accompanied by the use of ski poles with adjustable lengths for enhanced stability.
